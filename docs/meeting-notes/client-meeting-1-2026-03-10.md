Discussion & Decisions 

Project Background & Current Process 

The client, Daniela, provided an overview of the current workload management process. 

Current Workflow: Data is manually gathered from various sources (e.g., unit reports and staff information) and consolidated into a school-wide master spreadsheet. 

Identified Pain Points: The manual process is inefficient, prone to errors, and requires administrative staff to send individual emails or screenshots to academics for verification. 

 

Key Functional Requirements 

Automated Data Consolidation: The system must be able to pull and merge data from multiple source templates into a centralized database. 

FTE & Leave Validation Logic: * The system should automatically verify if workload hours align with an academic's FTE (e.g., 1.0 FTE = 37.5 hours/week).  

If an academic has more than 6 weeks of leave, this must be reflected in the system's calculations. 

Online Query System: Academics should be able to submit change requests directly within the system, which then require approval from the Head of Department (HoD) or Line Manager. 

 

Confidentiality & Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) 

Privacy: Strict confidentiality is required. Academics must only be able to see their own workload data and cannot see anyone else's information. 

Access Levels: * Admins: Full access to all data within the school. 

Heads of Department (HoD): Access to view data for academics within their specific department only. 

Academics: View-only access to their own records with the ability to submit queries.
